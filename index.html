Helpful Answers

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for parents and students who want admission clarity before visiting the academy office.

How do I apply for admission online?
Fill in the enrollment form, upload the required passport size picture, and submit the form online. The academy team will review it and contact you with the next step.
How can I check my enrollment status?
Use the status check section with your CNIC number and date of birth in DD/MM/YYYY format to view whether your application is pending, confirmed, or rejected.
When should I visit the academy office?
Once your admission is confirmed, download the form and visit the admin office with the printed copy and the required fee during office timing.
Can I view results and notices online?
Yes. Results and announcements are published on the website, so students and parents can stay informed without waiting for manual updates.
